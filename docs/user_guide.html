Mastering Tracly

Welcome to the official Tracly User Guide. This comprehensive resource is designed to help you set up your inventory, manage multi-branch operations, and streamline your billing workflow.

1. Introduction

Tracly is a professional-grade Inventory and Billing System built for modern retail businesses. Whether you are managing a single shop or a multi-branch enterprise, Tracly provides the precision and reliability you need.

Core Philosophy: We believe in speed. Every part of Tracly is optimized for rapid data entry and instant reporting.

2. Getting Started

System Requirements

  • Node.js 18.x or higher (LTS recommended)
  • Any modern evergreen browser (Chrome, Edge, Firefox)
  • Minimum 4GB RAM for optimal performance

Initial Setup

  1. Clone the repository from GitHub.
  2. Install dependencies in both /client and /server folders using npm install.
  3. Start the database and server using npm start.
  4. Access the dashboard at http://localhost:4200.

3. Admin Features

The Administrator panel is the command center of your business. From here, you can manage users, configure tenants, and set global business rules.

  • Role-Based Access: Define permissions for Admins and Cashiers.
  • Tenant Isolation: Manage multiple business entities on one installation.
  • Global Settings: Setup tax rates, currency symbols, and branding logos.

4. Inventory Management

Maintain complete control over your product catalog. Tracly supports massive inventories with lightning-fast search capabilities.

Adding a Product

Navigate to the Inventory section and click "Add New Product". You can define SKUs, barcodes, cost prices, and retail prices instantly.

Pro Tip: Use the "Bulk Import" feature to upload thousands of products via a simple CSV template.

5. Billing & Invoicing

Our billing terminal is designed for high-traffic environments. Process sales in seconds with automated tax calculations and discount profiling.

  • Barcode Ready: Connect any USB/Bluetooth scanner for instant scanning.
  • Thermal Printing: Direct support for 80mm and 58mm thermal printers.
  • Dynamic Discounts: Apply flat or percentage discounts at the line-item or bill-wide level.

6. Multi-Branch Operations

Scale your business across multiple locations. Tracly tracks stock movements between branches with full audit trails.

Stock Transfers

Initiate transfers from your main warehouse to specific retail nodes. The system locks inventory during transit to ensure data integrity.

7. Reports & Analytics

Turn your data into insights. Tracly provides real-time reporting on every aspect of your business.

  • Daily Sales: Track revenue performance hour-by-hour.
  • Product Velocity: See which items are flying off the shelves.
  • Tax Liability: Accurate GST/VAT reports for easy filing.

8. Troubleshooting

Need help? Here are common solutions to typical setup questions.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Logo not appearing? Check the /uploads permissions on your server.
  • Database error? Ensure SQLite binaries are correctly installed for your OS.
  • Printer not working? Verify the thermal driver is set to the correct COM port.
